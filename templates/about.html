About Hex

Player's take turn filling in hexagons with their colour. The object of the game is to connect the opposite sides of your colour.

For example, if you are red then you are trying to connect the top left side to the bottom right side with an unbroken line of red hexagons. Your opponent is trying to stop you, and at the same time trying to connect his blue sides.

Only one of you can win, but one of you will win. For more information and some basic strategy, see the Wikipedia page.
